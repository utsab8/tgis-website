About TGIS Nepal

The Geographical Information System Nepal

Our Story

TGIS Nepal Pvt. Ltd., based in Kathmandu, is a newly established company built on a strong foundation of over two decades of surveying, engineering, and IT expertise. Our team has successfully delivered high-quality services in topographic surveys, DGPS surveys, contour mapping, wind and solar energy plant surveys, national highway and bridge detail surveys, layout marking, and utility pipeline surveys. Alongside our surveying work, we provide comprehensive IT solutions — including GIS software development, geospatial data management, drone-based data processing, and custom technology integrations — to meet the evolving needs of infrastructure and development projects in Nepal.

At TGIS Nepal Pvt. Ltd., we combine the latest innovations in GIS, drone mapping, data analysis, and IT systems with the deep knowledge and precision gained through years of successful projects. Our mission is simple — to deliver accurate, reliable, and timely solutions for both civil engineering and IT-based development projects, ensuring complete client satisfaction. Though new in name, we carry forward a legacy of excellence and are committed to shaping the future of surveying, geospatial, and technology services in Nepal from our base in Kathmandu.
