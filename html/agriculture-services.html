Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our agricultural services

What size farms can benefit from precision agriculture?

Farms of all sizes can benefit from precision agriculture. While larger operations may see more immediate ROI on certain technologies, we offer scalable solutions that can be tailored to small and medium-sized farms as well. Our team works with you to identify the most cost-effective solutions for your specific operation.

How often should soil analysis be performed?

For most crops and soil types, we recommend comprehensive soil analysis every 2-3 years, with targeted sampling in problem areas annually. However, this can vary based on crop rotation, soil type, and management practices. Our agronomists can help develop a customized soil sampling plan for your operation.

What types of crops can be monitored with your services?

Our crop monitoring services can be applied to virtually any crop type, including row crops (corn, soybeans, wheat), specialty crops (fruits, vegetables), orchards, vineyards, and more. The specific technologies and methodologies may vary by crop type, but our fundamental approach works across all agricultural production systems.

How quickly can I see ROI from implementing precision agriculture?

Many clients see ROI within the first growing season, particularly with variable rate applications and irrigation management. More comprehensive systems may take 1-2 seasons to show full financial returns. We work with clients to prioritize implementations that will provide the quickest returns while building toward long-term sustainability and profitability.
